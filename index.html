Who we are

Bespoke, person-centred care, built around each young person.

BremCare was established to deliver targeted mentoring and support for vulnerable young people. We provide a safe, supportive and nurturing environment for those formally accommodated by a local authority, helping them build the skills, confidence and relationships to lead independent, fulfilling lives.

  • Ring-fenced shared and solo accommodation
  • 24-hour support with key workers who live on the property
  • A holistic approach to mentoring, education and wellbeing
  • Close, joined-up working with placing local authorities

Our promise

More than a place to stay — a place to belong.

Every young person deserves to feel safe, valued and loved. We take the time to truly know each one — to listen, to celebrate the good days and to be a steady, dependable presence on the harder ones.

Our key workers live alongside the young people they support, sharing mealtimes and marking milestones together. Because feeling cared for and believed in is where growing up well begins.
