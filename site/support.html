Who we support

Young people taking their next step.

We provide bespoke, person-centred support for vulnerable young people aged 16 and over, tailored to each individual’s needs and the goals they’re working towards.

Our client groups

The young people we work with.

16+

Looked-after children

Young people aged 16 and over, accommodated by a local authority under Section 20 or Section 31 of the Children Act 1989.

Vulnerable

Particularly vulnerable

Young people judged as particularly vulnerable by children’s social care and needing a stable, supportive home.

YOS

Youth offending referrals

Young people referred through youth offending services, supported to make positive, socially responsible choices.

Our services

A flexible range of support.

  • Solo placements
  • Floating support
  • Personal care and daily living
  • Overnight sleep-in and waking-night support
  • Sitting and companionship support
  • Overseeing medication
  • Food and meal preparation
  • Shopping and collecting prescriptions
  • Support with learning disabilities
  • Attending appointments and escorted outings
  • Administrative tasks and form-filling
  • Laundry and general housework
Levels of support

Support that flexes to need.

🛒

Floating support

Planned support hours for young people developing their independence, with a key worker on hand.

🤵

2:1 and 3:1 support

Higher-intensity, structured support for young people who need closer guidance day to day.

🌙

24-hour and waking night

Round-the-clock support with sleep-in or waking-night staff who live on the property.
