Safer recruitment

What to expect when you apply.

We follow government guidance on safe recruitment, so every step protects the young people in our care.

  • Application form and full employment history
  • Interview, including a discussion about safeguarding
  • Written references obtained and verified
  • Enhanced DBS criminal records check
  • Qualifications verified where relevant
  • A probationary period and full safeguarding training
