About BremCare

Born out of passion and compassion.

We deliver bespoke, client-centred mentoring and support for vulnerable young people — keeping each young person at the forefront of everything we do.

Our purpose

An outcomes-focused organisation, built around the young person.

Those who come through the care system often have multiple needs that must be identified and addressed to reduce the likelihood of poor outcomes later in life. We provide a safe, supportive and nurturing environment for young people aged 16 and over who are accommodated by a local authority.

We offer ring-fenced shared and solo accommodation and are registered with Ofsted as a provider of supported accommodation. Our service is designed solely with the child in mind, offering 24-hour support with key workers who live on the property.

A safe, settled place to call home

“We empower and strengthen young people’s practical, social and emotional skills, preparing them to lead independent and fulfilling lives.”

Aims & objectives

A strong value base, with young people at the centre.

BremCare operates within a clear set of aims that guide every placement and every decision.

🛡

🤝

Positive relationships

To build trusting relationships and support successful reintegration into communities.

🌱

Growth & maturity

To develop self-confidence, self-esteem and independence in every young person.

🧠

Resilience & skills

To build self-control, resilience, critical thinking and pro-social life skills.

📝

To promote anti-discriminatory attitudes and meet each young person’s cultural needs.

Mentoring that nurtures every area of growth
A holistic approach

Mentoring is at the heart of BremCare.

We offer a unique, holistic approach to mentoring. Beyond regular consultations, we make sure every area of personal growth is nurtured — employment, education, relationship-building and a positive mindset.

  • Regular key-work sessions and a weekly residents’ meeting
  • One-to-one and group work to support open, honest discussion
  • Weekly activities that build talents and confidence
  • Joined-up working with local authority professionals
Education, training & employment

An investment in the future.

We recognise that investing in young people’s education and employment is an investment in our whole community. We support this through private tuition, attending school meetings, and collaborating with businesses to create work experience and youth-friendly opportunities.

Cultural diversity

Celebrating unique differences.

BremCare is committed to equality of opportunity, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, ethnicity, nationality, disability or race. We celebrate difference and help young people build the community, social and family networks that strengthen identity and resilience.
