Safeguarding

Keeping young people safe is the foundation of everything.

🛡

Child-centred service

Our service is designed solely with the child in mind, with 24-hour support from key workers who live on the property.

🗣

An open-ear policy

All issues are taken seriously and never dismissed. Young people can always raise concerns about their stay.

📝

Clear complaints route

Every young person receives a guide explaining internal and external complaints routes, with access to a telephone.

🤝

Young people heard

Regular key-work sessions, a weekly residents’ meeting and monthly meetings with senior managers.

📊

Reviews & monitoring

Monthly review meetings monitor each young person’s growth plan and progress.

🔒

Confidentiality

Privacy is protected throughout, with information shared only with those who need to know.

Safer recruitment

The right people, recruited the right way.

All staff and volunteers are appointed, trained, supported and supervised in line with government guidance on safe recruitment.

  • Enhanced DBS criminal records checks
  • Written references obtained and verified before appointment
  • Safeguarding discussed at interview
  • Qualifications verified and a probationary period completed
  • Every staff member given our safeguarding policy
Training & development

Well-trained, well-supported staff.

In their first year, staff complete mandatory training in safer caring, safeguarding, positive behaviour, first aid, de-escalation, e-safety and more.

  • At least 25 hours of training every year
  • Ongoing programmes that improve outcomes for young people
  • Preparation for independent living, drug and health awareness
  • Delivered by our director and contracted specialists
